Why EcoSphere

Built like an ERP, scored like a scoreboard.

Operational, not just reported

Carbon is calculated automatically from real transactions — electricity, fuel, fleet, purchases — not entered by hand at quarter-end.

Governance you can prove

Policy acknowledgement, internal audits, corrective actions and a risk register, all with owners and due dates.

Gamified participation

XP, badges, coins and leaderboards turn CSR and sustainability targets into something employees actually engage with.

AI-assisted insight

Score prediction, anomaly detection, and a recommendation engine surface what needs attention before it becomes a finding.

Three pillars, one platform

Environmental. Social. Governance.

Environmental

Emission factors, carbon transactions, fleet & manufacturing emissions, waste, water, renewables, and goal tracking.

Social

CSR activities, volunteering, diversity metrics, training, and manager-approved evidence uploads.

Governance

Policies, internal audits, compliance issues, corrective actions, and a live risk register.
